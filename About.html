Welcome To Nostaliga Online Bookstore


Nostaliga is an online platform created to provide individuals with access to scientific research and literature. Nostaliga arose out of the idea of ​​building a more knowledgeable world, where there was never a time when easy and affordable access to content was most needed, and so were all kinds of books.Current scientific databases are behind paid walls that are disrupted or require easy access and this is a challenge for individuals around the world who are not affiliated with organizations. Access to scientific publications is particularly troublesome in developing countries, where many universities cannot afford to subscribe to the databases required to advance research and studies.The mission of the Online Library is to develop and maintain online resources and services to support the current and future teaching, learning and research needs of the distance learning community.Nostaliga removes barriers of scientific discovery to make academic literature affordable and accessible to all, all academic and non-academic professionals, students, and knowledge lovers.
